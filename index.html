Contact a healthcare provider

Use maternity triage or urgent services for:

  • Vaginal bleeding
  • Abdominal pain
  • Watery or clear discharge
  • Reduced fetal movement, when relevant
  • Fever or severe vomiting
  • Headache or visual disturbance
  • Any other concern
Do not wait for a reply in this app if you feel seriously unwell.
