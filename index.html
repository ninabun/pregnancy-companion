Contact a healthcare provider

Please approach your hospital or obstetric department for:

  • Vaginal bleeding
  • Abdominal pain
  • Watery or clear discharge
  • Reduced fetal movement, when relevant
  • Fever or severe vomiting
  • Headache or visual disturbance
  • Any other clinical concern
Do not rely on this app for urgent or clinical assessment.
